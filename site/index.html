Features

The agent OS underneath it all

Battle-tested patterns from daily use. Each layer is designed to work alone or as part of the full stack.

🎭

Agent Identity

A bootstrap conversation lets your assistant discover its own name, creature type, and communication style. It becomes yours.

🔄

Domain Routing

One assistant, eight clean domains. ROLES.md routes context by trigger words so your grocery list never leaks into a standup summary.

📚

Persistent Memory

Daily session notes, long-term curated memory, and heartbeat state. Your assistant remembers what matters across sessions.

🔔

Proactive Automation

Departure reminders with real-time driving ETA, CRM nudges, appointment alerts, email triage, and weather checks.

🔗

Cross-Referencing

Files link to each other with → pointers. One source of truth, many references — a shared school file serves multiple kids.

💬

Group Chat Aware

Knows when to speak up and when to stay quiet. Responds when mentioned or adding value, stays silent for banter. Memory stays private.

🛡️

Safety Guardrails

No exfiltration of private data, no destructive commands without asking, prefers trash over rm. Built-in from day one.

📱

Platform Formatting

Auto-adapts output for Discord, WhatsApp, and chat. No markdown tables in mobile, link embeds suppressed, headers adjusted.

🛠️

Scripts & Skills

Driving ETA via Google Directions API, audio transcription, gog CLI for Gmail & Calendar. Extensible by design.

Quick Start

Up and running in 5 minutes

No dependencies. No build step. Just Markdown files and your OpenClaw workspace.

1

Clone the templates

Copy the templates/ folder into your OpenClaw workspace. Rename files to drop the .template suffix.

2

Bootstrap your assistant

Open BOOTSTRAP.md and start a conversation. Your assistant will discover its own name, creature type, and emoji.

3

Add your context

Fill in USER.md with your details and ROLES.md to define your work/home boundaries.

4

Pick your domains

Add the domain files you need — family, household, work, health, finances, travel, hobbies, people. Start with one or two and expand over time.

5

Wire up automation

Enable heartbeats, departure reminders, and scripts. Your assistant is now proactive, not just reactive.
