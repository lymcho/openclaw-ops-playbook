# OpenClaw Ops Playbook

**A complete agent architecture for running your entire life through one AI assistant.**

Most AI assistant setups stop at "here's a prompt." This playbook gives you 30+ ready-to-use templates covering agent identity, family, work, household, health, finances, travel, and hobbies — with domain routing that knows when you're in work mode vs family mode.

---

## Who This Is For

Busy professionals managing families, careers, side gigs, and everything in between. You already run OpenClaw locally and want a structured way to organize your life through it.

---

## What You Get

### Agent Foundation
- **Bootstrap** — First-run conversation to discover your assistant's identity
- **SOUL + IDENTITY** — Personality, values, and communication style
- **AGENTS.md** — The operating system: session protocol, file architecture, memory rules

### Domain Routing
- **ROLES.md** — 7 life domains (family, household, work, health, finances, travel, hobbies) with automatic context switching
- Cross-referenced files: one source of truth, many pointers

### Family
- Per-child files (activities, school, medical, friends, milestones)
- Shared school files (calendar, contacts, policies)
- Partner, extended family, and pet templates

### Household
- Vendor management (babysitters, cleaners, contractors, mechanics)
- Home maintenance schedules and appliance warranties
- Vehicle tracking, subscriptions, and all insurance policies

### Work
- Multi-engagement support (full-time job + side gigs + content creation)
- Professional knowledge base that feeds across all engagements
- Idea inbox and quick wins

### Health, Finances, Travel
- Family medical hub with appointment tracking
- Operating budget with assistant-enforceable spending rules
- Investment accounts, tax prep checklists, estate planning
- Travel profiles with loyalty programs and passport tracking

### Automation
- Departure reminders with real-time driving ETA
- CRM check-ins with relationship cadence
- Heartbeat patterns for proactive behavior

---

## Quick Start

1. Copy `templates/` into your OpenClaw workspace
2. Start with `BOOTSTRAP.md`
3. Fill in `USER.md` and `ROLES.md`
4. Add one domain at a time
5. Wire up automation when ready

Full walkthrough: [product/playbook.md](https://github.com/lymcho/openclaw-ops-playbook/blob/main/product/playbook.md)
