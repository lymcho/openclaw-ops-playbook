## How This Works

**Adding someone:** "Add [name] to my CRM, [cadence], [context]"

**Check-in reminders:** During heartbeats, I'll flag anyone overdue and give you context to make it easy.

**After reaching out:** Tell me "messaged [name]" → I update last contact and recalculate next due.

**Snooze:** "Snooze [name] 2 weeks" → pushes the reminder without resetting the cadence.

**Draft help:** "Draft a message to [name]" → I'll write something based on their notes.

## Cadence Guide

- **Weekly** — Close friends, active collaborators
- **2 weeks** — Good friends, work buddies
- **Monthly** — Extended network, mentors
- **Quarterly** — Loose ties, old colleagues
- **Milestone** — Birthday/life event only (no regular cadence)
