## How This Works

When planning a trip, I pull from this file for active bookings and from `travel/profiles.md` for loyalty numbers and preferences. As the trip approaches, I'll run through the pre-trip checklist during heartbeats.

After a trip, move it to Past Trips with a quick review for future reference.
