## How This Works

When you need a vendor — "who's our plumber?" or "book the babysitter for Saturday" — I check this file. I can also remind you about recurring services (annual HVAC tune-up, quarterly deep clean) during heartbeats.

**Adding vendors:** Tell me about a new vendor and I'll add them here.
**Rating vendors:** After a service, note if they were good/bad so we remember for next time.
