## Seasonal Schedule

### Spring (March - May)

- [ ] HVAC: Schedule AC tune-up
- [ ] Gutters: Clean out winter debris
- [ ] Exterior: Check for winter damage, touch up paint
- [ ] Landscaping: Spring planting, mulch
- [ ] Windows: Clean inside and out
- [ ] Smoke/CO detectors: Test and replace batteries
- [ ] Pest control: Spring treatment

### Summer (June - August)

- [ ] HVAC: Replace air filters (monthly in summer)
- [ ] Deck / patio: Seal or stain if needed
- [ ] Irrigation: Check sprinkler system
- [ ] Appliances: Clean refrigerator coils
- [ ] Dryer vent: Professional cleaning

### Fall (September - November)

- [ ] HVAC: Schedule heating tune-up
- [ ] Gutters: Clean before winter
- [ ] Weatherstripping: Check doors and windows
- [ ] Furnace filter: Replace
- [ ] Outdoor: Winterize irrigation, store furniture
- [ ] Chimney: Inspect and clean (if applicable)
- [ ] Snow removal: Confirm service contract

### Winter (December - February)

- [ ] Pipes: Monitor for freezing risk
- [ ] Ice dams: Monitor roof
- [ ] HVAC filters: Replace monthly
- [ ] Deep clean: Schedule interior deep clean
- [ ] Tax prep: Gather home-related deductions
