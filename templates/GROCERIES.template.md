## How This Works

**Adding items:** Tell me what you need. I'll check preferences and add to the right store list(s). Unknown items → I'll ask or default to "Any Store."

**At the store:** Text the store name (or auto-trigger via iOS Shortcut). I'll reply with that store's list.

**After shopping:** Tell me what you bought. I'll cross them off.

**Learning:** If you consistently buy something at a specific store, I'll update preferences.
